Red, Blue, Yellow "Eggs"

*not sure what they’re exactly called*

Go to Konohagakure and proceed to the yellow location.

You’ll see 3 sets of of red, blue, and yellow eggs.

Set the eggs at the markings located near the area.

Once everything has been set in place, it’ll start raining Level 1 Magatamas.

Important notes:

  • If you repeat the color of one egg, you’ll get Ramens instead of Magatamas (example: blue + blue + red = Ramen)
  • One set consumes 3 big world points but you may repeat the RBY eggs 4 times with the 4th giving less magatamas
  • The eggs respawns after 5 minutes and will disappear when you are carrying one
