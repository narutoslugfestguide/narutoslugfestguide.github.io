Red, Blue, Yellow Eggs

*not sure what they’re exactly called*

Go to Konohagakure and proceed to the yellow location.

You’ll see 3 sets of of red, blue, and yellow eggs.

Set the eggs at the markings located near the area.

Once everything has been set in place, it’ll start raining Level 1 Magatamas. (Level 40)

Important notes:

  • If you double the color of one egg, you’ll get Ramens instead of Magatamas (example: blue, blue, red = Ramen)
  • One set consumes 3 big world points
  • Eggs respawns 5 minutes after consumed
