Find Kitten

Look through Boxes near Konoha streets

Auto-navigate feature is disabled. You need to run around the Konoha street looking for boxes that glimmers. They are all over the place. My go to box is the one located at Konohagakure Property. In front of the Love Temple. Under the street lantern post.

You just need to run through or attack the boxes. There is a high chance a kitten will appear and you would have completed your bounty.
