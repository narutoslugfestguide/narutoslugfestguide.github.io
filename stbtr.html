Strange Things by the River

Part 1: Head to the Land of Rivers 0/1

Part 2: Carry fish into the river 0/6

Part 1: Investigate the cause of the anomaly

You can press the quest and let the auto-navigate take you to the location. It is important that you let the auto-navigate finish.

You will know that the auto-navigate has finished when the quest has change from “Head to the Land of Rivers 0/1” to “Carry fish into the river 1/6“.


Part 2: Carry fish into the river 1/6

You need to carry those fishes on the land into to the water holes. There are a total of 6 fishes that you can carry into any of the 3 water holes.

You will need to drop the fishes into any of the water holes.

Do this for 6 times and you’ll finish your quest.


Bonus:You’ll receive Ramen from completing the Big World quests
