Collect Bug

Collect 3 bugs 0/3

Use rocks to break the tree trunks on the ground

Find a log which are scattered across the maps of Land of Fire (Konohagakure) and Land of Rivers. There will always be a rock nearby.

Smash the log using the rock to collect the bugs. Do this for 3 times.

Once you have all 3 bugs, press the quest to let auto-navigate take you to Shino's location and submit the quest.
