Arrest Fake Daimyo

Arrest Fake Daimyo bounty will be posted at the Bounty Handbook at exactly 00:00 , 10:00 and 18:00 (GMT+8) Up to 2 Arrest Fake Daimyo posts can spawn at the bounty board Each post can be claim 10 times. Players may claim the bounty more than once. See the “Remaining” at the lower right of the picture for remaining amount. Arrest Fake Daimyo cannot be done with party but is very easy to accomplish.

Guide

You will be teleported inside those strange things where you will encounter the soldiers of the fake daimyo

There is a total of 4 waves with the last wave being the fake daimyo.

The soldier’s level do not adjust to your level. I’am level 55 here but the soldier are still level 30. They can easily be killed with 1 or 2 hits/spells/partner.

The fake daimyo will then appear at the last wave and since he is only level 1, 1 or 2 hits is enough to kill him.

Important notes:

  • The moment you killed the fake daimyo at the end is only the time you will know that you selected the correct location if the bounty was successfully completed.
  • If you are fast enough, you can finish more than 1 fake daimyo bounty per day.
  • If you are too slow, you might not even be able to get the bounty.
  • This bounty has a 2 hour time limit upon taking.
