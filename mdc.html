Making Delicious Curry

Part 1: Go to Granny’s curry pot 0/1

Part 2: Create delicious Curry Rice 0/1

Click on the quest to let the auto-navigate feature take you the location at the Land of Rivers.

Let the auto-navigate finish to turn the quest from “Go to Granny’s curry pot 0/1” to “Create delicious Curry Rice 0/1”.

Put the ingredients at the 3 rock slabs located near the cooking pot. In any order: bucket of rice, basket of food, and red/yellow curry.

Once all the ingredients are in place, the cooking pot will boil and after a second a curry will be available for you to eat to finish the quest.

Note: If red curry doesn’t work, try the yellow curry next.
