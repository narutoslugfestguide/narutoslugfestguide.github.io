Rescue Jiraiya

Rescue Jiraiya bounty will be posted at the Bounty Handbook at exactly 00:00 , 10:00 and 18:00 (GMT+8). Only 1 Rescue Jiraiya will be posted each time. The post can be claim 10 times. Players may claim the bounty more than once. See the “Remaining” at the lower right of the picture for remaining amount. Rescue Jiraiya can be done with or without a party.

Once you have accepted the quest, you can press the bounty and auto-navigate will take you to the location which is at Amegakure.

The Amegakure mobs will match your level which means they can be tough to beat and deal a lot damage.

You can rush towards where the bombs are located and use them to defeat the first and second Amegakure mobs.

The second wave will have an Amegakure Captain included which can stun you and make you release your bomb

You may actually defeat all the Amegakure mobs using the bombs including the boss but will take time.

After the first 2 sets of Amegakure mobs, you’ll encounter these 2 Amegakure Inspectors. You can use the bomb located nearby to easily defeat them.

Upon reaching the top of the stairs you will encounter another set of mobs with an Amegakure Captain.

You can easily defeat them through normal combat or you may use the bombs again.

You will then encounter an Amegakure Jonin which you can either avoid by jumping and passing at the side ledge.

You may also attract the Amegakure Jonin and have him follow you to where the bombs are located, making it easier to explode him.

After avoiding or defeating the Amegakure Jonin, you will encounter 2 Amegakure Raiders before the final boss both can be easily defeated through normal combat.

Finally, as you arrive at the final boss platform, you will only see a frog.

This frog will eventually turn into the Amegakure Leader who is both tanky and can deal a lot of damage.

It's up to you if you want to fight him or bring a bomb all the way to his area. Either both options will take time.

Should you choose to use the bomb option, you will need to defeat all the mobs including the Amegakure Jonin to not risk dropping the bomb.
