Obtain intel

Part 1: Go to Land of Wind 0/1

Part 2: Eavesdrop Enemy Intel 0/1

Click on the quest to let the auto-navigate feature take you the location at the Land of Wind.

You will have to let the auto-navigate finish to turn the quest from “Go to Land of Wind 0/1″ to “Eavesdrop Enemy Intel 0/1“.

You also know that the quest has started when you enter scene mode and your character transform into a Sunagakure Ninja.

You’ll see 3 different types of ninjas:

Sunagakure Ninja Scout – Those with the red life and red circles and are constantly roaming. They are extremely powerful and will kill you with one blow.

Sunagakure Sand Ninja – Those with no circles and with blue life. They are just dummies to confuse you.

Rogue Ninja – This is the ninja that you need to find to complete the quest.

You’ll need to find the Rogue Ninja. He is usually at the edge of the quest area. He will be beside a Sunagakure Sand Ninja.

Tip: Sunagakure Ninja Scouts are extremely slow. Even if you get caught, you just need to simply outrun the until you find the Rogue Ninja

Once you approach the Rogue Ninja and the Sunagakure Ninja, they’ll start a conversation and your quest would have been completed.

Note: If you die from the Sunagakure Ninja Scouts and fail your quest, you can always tap on the quest to start again.
